Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our electrical services and processes.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation quotes for all electrical services. Our experienced electricians will assess your project and provide a detailed estimate with transparent pricing. Contact us today to schedule your free consultation and see why Humboldt County trusts Trinidad Electric.

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

We offer 24/7 emergency service throughout Humboldt County, typically responding within 1-2 hours for urgent electrical issues. Electrical emergencies can be dangerous and require immediate attention. Our licensed electricians are always on call to ensure your safety and restore power quickly.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We hold CA State License #681965 C10 C46 and are fully insured for your protection and peace of mind. Our certifications ensure all work meets current NEC standards and local building codes. We're also bonded, providing additional protection for our customers.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Humboldt County, with offices in Arcata, a construction yard in Manila, and our owner based in Trinidad. This strategic positioning allows us to provide prompt service throughout the region, from emergency calls to planned installations.

What types of electrical work do you perform?

We handle all aspects of electrical work including residential and commercial installations, panel upgrades, code compliance, emergency repairs, new construction wiring, and lighting installations. Whether it's a simple outlet repair or a complete electrical system overhaul, our experienced team has you covered.

Do you offer warranty on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties on both labor and materials. Our commitment to quality ensures that your electrical work will be done right the first time. We also provide ongoing support and maintenance to keep your electrical systems operating safely and efficiently.
