Our Mission

It is our mission to provide our customers with electrical service whether they are business owners, homeowners, landlords, tenants, or general or other types of contractors, while always maintaining the standards of the National Electric Code (NEC).

Code compliant installations are our priority. We believe that safe, professional electrical work isn't just what we do—it's who we are.

Meet Grant Lay, Owner & NEC Expert

Owner Grant Lay brings exceptional expertise to every project. As an Instructor for College of the Redwoods, Grant teaches a variety of electrical classes including one which focuses specifically on the National Electric Code (NEC).

This educational background ensures that every Trinidad Electric project meets the highest standards of safety and compliance. When you choose Trinidad Electric, you're getting the expertise of someone who literally teaches the electrical code.

Grant lives in Trinidad, ensuring our local roots run deep and our commitment to the community is personal.
